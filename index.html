Approach Explained
Card 1 has Initial Position Absolute and Cards 2,3,4 have Initial Position Fixed
As you Scroll Down the position property of these cards updates from Absolute to Fixed for card 1 and from Fixed -> Abosulte ->Fixed for cars 2,3,4
As you Scroll Up the reverse happens to maintain the Card Stack
CSS Classes have been made havinf initial, next and final positon and Js funtions ADD and Remove these classes depending on the Scroll Value of Y-Axis
